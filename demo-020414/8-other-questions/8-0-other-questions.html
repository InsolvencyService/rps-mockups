Additional information about your claim

Do you want to claim compensation for loss of notice?

You can claim compensation if your employer has failed to give you the minimum statutory notice.

You are expected to keep your loss to a minimum by doing your best to find a new job or by claiming benefits, such as jobseekers allowance. You will be emailed a link to an online form to complete your claim form for notice pay at the end of your statutory notice period.

Have you been awarded any payments from a Employment tribunal in relation to your job ending?

You will be asked to send a copy of the tribunal award with your claim form. If you made a settlement through ACAS or a compromise agreement with your employer, You will be asked to send a copy of the agreement.
