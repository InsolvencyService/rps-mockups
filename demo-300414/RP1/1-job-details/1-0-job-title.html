3. What category of worker were you?
What do these categories mean?
EmployedRegardless of the hours you worked or where you worked, you were employed under a contract of employment
Director/shareholderYou were an office holder of the company
Labour-only sub-contractorYou were self employed and paid tax and national insurance on that basis
Freelance workerYou worked for yourself and the business was your client
Agency workerYou were working for the business on behalf of an agency
Casual workerYou worked for the employer as and when required
Fixed-term contracts workerYour contract was for a specific period of time i.e. it had an end date
Home workerYou worked from home but attended an office for meetings
