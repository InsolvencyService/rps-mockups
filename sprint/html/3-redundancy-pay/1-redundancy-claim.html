Details of the redundancy claim

Section 3 of 6


This section of your claim is all about redundancy pay.

You are entitled to receive Redundancy Pay if: you worked for your employer for more than two years or the employer has gone out of business and they didn't already pay you your Redundancy Pay entitlement.

You can use the following link to calculate your statutory redundancy pay which will give you an idea of how much you might be entitled to. Calculate your statutory redundancy pay

What are the questions in section 3?
  1. Would you like to claim for redundancy pay?
  2. When did you write to your employer or an Employment Tribunal about the redundancy pay you were owed?
  3. Did you get a redundancy payment from Digital Testing Two Limited?
  4. How much was the redundancy payment?

If you choose 'No' you won't need to answer any questions in section 3.
