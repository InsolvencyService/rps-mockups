Section 4 of 6

Details of holiday pay

Holiday pay is calculated pro-rata up to the day you were made redundant. You will not be entitled to a full holiday year if you have not worked a full year. You can claim up to a maximum of 6 weeks worth of holiday pay.

This section of your claim will ask about your holiday entitlement at the job from which you were made redundant. The following information will help you with the questions in section 4:

  • details of your yearly holiday entitlement amount of holiday taken this holiday year
What are the questions in section 4?
  1. Are you owed any holiday pay?
  2. What was the start date of your holiday year at Digital Testing Two Limited?
  3. How many days holiday per year were you entitled to?
  4. Does this number of holiday days include bank holidays?
  5. Did you carry any holiday days forward from last year's holiday allowance into this year?
  6. How many untaken holiday days have you carried forward to this holiday year?
  7. How many holiday days have you taken this holiday year, including bank holidays?

If you choose 'No' you won't need to answer any questions in section 4.
