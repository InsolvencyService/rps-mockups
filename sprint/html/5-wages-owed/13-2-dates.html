Dates of wages owed

You can add more than one period for wages owed up to a maximum of 8 weeks. There is an option to add more periods on the summary screen, but this should only be used if you are sure you have more periods to add. You can't add part weeks together to make a whole week: if you choose a week in which you are only owed one day of pay this will count as a whole week in your 8 week allocation.

This includes any failed payments to your bank account or bounced cheques from your employer.
