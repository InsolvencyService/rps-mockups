Thank you for submitting your loss of notice claim

If we need to contact you we will use carolynwarburton@hotmail.com

What we will do next

We are processing your claim and will send you an acknowledgment that your claim has been received. You should receive an email in the next 72 hours.

You can expect to receive payment within 10-15 days.

What you can do next

  • If you were self employed please send a copy of your Trading, profit and loss account to us, labelling itcarefully with your name, address and National Insurance number
  • Please contact us if you have changed your bank account since you were paid your redundancy or insolvency payments
  • Send email or post copies of your evidence to theRedundancy Payments service. We accept clear scans, digital images and photocopies. Include your name, address and National Insurance number
  • If you have any questions about your claim, contact the Redundancy Payments Service quoting your National Insurance Number
Finished

If you have any questions on what to do next, contact the Redundancy Payments Service.
