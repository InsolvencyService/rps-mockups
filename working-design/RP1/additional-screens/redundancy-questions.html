What are the questions in a redundancy claim?

  • Details of the job from which you were made redundant

    1. What was your job title?
    2. What was your employee, roll or works number?
    3. What category of worker were you?
    4. When did your employment with your employer start?
    5. What was the last day you worked for your employer?
    6. Was this the date your employment officially ended?
    7. When did your employment officially end?
    8. Did your employer give you notice?
    9. When did your employer give you notice?
    10. Do you want to claim compensation for loss of notice?

  • Details of wage and working hours

    1. What was your gross income at your employer?
    2. Did you receive any extra bonus or commission as part of your contract?
    3. What bonus or commission did you receive?
    4. Which day of the week were you paid?
    5. How many hours a week were you contracted to work?
    6. Did your contract state you were paid for overtime hours?
    7. How many overtime hours did you normally work?
    8. How many days did you normally work each week?
    9. Did you work Monday to Friday?
    10. Which days were you paid to work?

  • Details of the redundancy claim

    1. Would you like to claim for redundancy pay?
    2. Has your employer paid you part of your redundancy payment?
    3. How much of your redundancy payment did your employer pay?

  • Details of any breaks in employment

    1. Did you have any breaks in employment?
    2. When did you have a break in employment at your employer?
    3. What was the reason for this break in employment?

  • Details of holiday pay

    1. Are you owed any holiday pay?
    2. What was the start date of your holiday year at your employer?
    3. How many days holiday per year were you entitled to?
    4. Does this number of holiday days include bank holidays?
    5. Were you allowed to carry forward untaken holiday entitlement from your previous holiday year?
    6. How many untaken holiday days have you carried forward this year?
    7. How many holiday days have you taken this holiday year, including bank holidays?
    8. Dates of holiday owed
    9. How many days did you take as holiday in this period?

  • Details of wages owed

    1. Are you owed any wages?
    2. Were your wages subject to an attachment of earnings?
    3. How many days did you work without being paid?
    4. What is the gross amount of pay owed?

  • Details of new employment

    1. Have you had a new job since you were made redundant?
    2. New employer name
    3. New employer address
    4. Telephone number
    5. When were you offered this job?
    6. When did your employment with your new employer start?
    7. Although you have a different employer, is your new job role the same?
    8. Are you dealing with the same customers?
    9. Have your terms and conditions changed?
    10. Are you aware of any link between your employer and your new employer?
    11. What is the link between your employer and your new employer?

  • Other information about your claim

    1. Have you been awarded any payments from a Employment tribunal in relation to your job ending?
    2. Do you owe your employer any money?
    3. How much do you owe your employer?
    4. What is this money owed for?
    5. Is there anything else relating to this claim that you wish to tell us about?
