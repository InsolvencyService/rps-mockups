Claim a redundancy or insolvency payment

Apply if you’ve been made redundant and your employer can’t pay what you’re owed because they are insolvent.

You can claim for:

  • redundancy pay
  • holiday pay
  • wages owed

You can also apply for compensation for loss of notice.

What you need to know

Start now

  • Before you start
  • What you need to know

You should only use this service if:

  • your employment has been terminated by your employer
  • your National Insurance number
  • your employment details including dates and how much you were paid
  • your bank or building society details

You may also need to know any dates you were on holiday or absent from work, details of wages you are owed and other money owed by and to your employer.
